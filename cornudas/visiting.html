Visiting The Hill of James Magee

The Hill of James Magee

The Cornudas Mountain Foundation, which relies solely on charitable contributions and volunteers for support, welcomes visitors to The Hill several times a year. The Hill is located in the desert 75 miles east of El Paso, Texas. The following dates have been scheduled for visits during 2011.

May 14, 2011
October 15, 2011

The Hill is open to groups numbering no more than twelve. We reserve the right to cancel visits if fewer than three individuals have registered.

Each visit is an expensive undertaking for our Foundation, requiring the hiring of outside help to prepare The Hill for the visit, to transport and accompany visitors during their visit, and finally, to close the buildings afterwards. This does not take into account the considerable cost of maintaining The Hill during the course of the year or our ongoing capital expenses for completing the fourth building. We, therefore, require a minimum contribution of $250 per visitor, $400 per couple. Registered students will receive a 50% discount. Donors giving $1,000 or more will receive a limited edition lithograph by James Magee. The Cornudas Mountain Foundation is a 501(c)(3) charitable organization. To the extent allowed by law, contributions are tax-deductible.

Trips begin in El Paso. Visitors will be informed of the time and place to meet in advance of their scheduled visit. Individuals interested in visiting The Hill may send an email to info@mageehill.com noting which date is preferable.
